Recipe #1 Apple Pie

  • Time: Prep 15 minute Cook Time 50 minutes 400 F
  • Serves: 8 Pie Slices
Ingredients Quantity
Apples 10 medium apples
Cornstarch 1 tablespoon
Cinnamon 1 tablespoon
Nutmeg 1/2 tablespoon
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Sugar 1 Cup
Flour 3/4 Cup
Brown Sugar 1/2 Cup
Butter 1/4 Stick

Step 1: Peel and core 10 Apples
Step 2: Mix the apples with sugar, cornstratch, and spices
Step 3: Spoon the apple mix into a pre-made pie shell
Step 4: Mix, flour butter, and brown sugar as the crumb topping and spoon it on top of the pie
Step 5: Bake the pie at 400 F for 50 minutes

Recipe #2 Hot Fudge Sundae

  • Time: Prep 15 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity

Recipe #3 Corned Beef and Cabbage

  • Time: Prep 15 minute Cook Time 3 Hours High Setting Crook Pot
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Corned Beef 3 lbs
Potatoes 4 Diced Large
Cabbage 1 Head Quartered
Carrots 2 Pounds Peeled and Sliced

Step 1: Add Corned Beef to the Crook Pot, add 4 cups of water
Step 2: Dice & Add Cabbage Potatoes, & Carrots to the Crook Pot
Step 3: Cook at Hi Setting for 3 Hours
Step 4: Serve Hot with Soda Bread
